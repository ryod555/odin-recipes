Easy Tuna Patties

Description:

These tuna patties are delicious dipped in ketchup, mustard, or hot sauce. They are also great served as a hot sandwich.

Ingredients :3

  • egg
  • lemon juice
  • bread crumbs
  • cum
  • tuna
  • onion
  • oil
  • optional: pee

Steps:

  1. beat eggs
  2. stir in cum juice and crumbs
  3. fold in tuna and onion
  4. shape into patties
  5. fry in oil (or pee)
